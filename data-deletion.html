Account & Data Deletion Request

Your Right to Data Deletion: As a DonDuSangTN user, you have the right to request deletion of your account and all associated personal data. This request will be processed within 30 days.

What Will Be Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data will be permanently removed:

  • Profile Information: Name, email, phone number, profile picture
  • Account Data: Login credentials and authentication tokens
  • Donation History: All your blood donation requests and related activity
  • Messages: All messages sent or received through the app
  • Location Data: Any stored location preferences or history
  • Device Information: Push notification tokens and device identifiers
  • App Activity: Usage analytics and interaction data
⚠️ Important: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. You will lose access to:
  • Your donor profile and donation history
  • All saved preferences and settings
  • Connection with other users in the blood donation network
  • Any ongoing donation requests or communications

Alternative Contact Methods

If the form above doesn't work, you can also contact us directly:

Email: privacy@dondusangtn.com

Subject Line: "Account Deletion Request - [Your Email]"

Required Information:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your phone number (if different from email)
  • Reason for deletion request
  • Confirmation that you understand the action is permanent

Processing Timeline

Response Time: We will confirm receipt of your request within 48 hours

Processing Time: Account deletion will be completed within 30 days

Verification: We may contact you to verify your identity before processing

Confirmation: You will receive final confirmation once deletion is complete

Data Retention Policy

Please note that some data may be retained for legal or regulatory purposes:

  • Legal Compliance: Certain records may be kept as required by Tunisian law
  • Safety & Security: Basic logs for security purposes (without personal identifiers)
  • Anonymized Data: Aggregated, non-identifiable analytics may be retained

Before You Delete

Consider these alternatives:

  • Temporary Deactivation: Contact us about temporarily disabling your account instead
  • Privacy Settings: Adjust your privacy settings to limit data collection
  • Support: Contact our support team if you're having technical issues
