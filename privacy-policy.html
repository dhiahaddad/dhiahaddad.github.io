Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 31, 2025

1. Introduction

DonDuSangTN ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our blood donation mobile application (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We may collect the following personal information:

  • Name and contact information
  • Blood type and medical information relevant to blood donation
  • Account credentials (email, password)
  • Profile pictures (optional)

2.2 Location Information

With your permission, we collect location data to:

  • Find nearby blood donation centers
  • Connect you with local donors and recipients
  • Provide location-based notifications for urgent blood needs
  • Display donation centers on the map

2.3 Device Information

We automatically collect certain device information including:

  • Device type, operating system, and version
  • Unique device identifiers
  • App usage data and crash reports

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for:

  • Facilitating blood donation connections
  • Sending important notifications about blood donation opportunities
  • Improving our services and user experience
  • Ensuring the safety and security of our platform
  • Complying with legal obligations

4. Permissions Explained

4.1 Location Access (ACCESS_FINE_LOCATION, ACCESS_COARSE_LOCATION)

Why we need it: To help you find nearby blood donation centers and connect with local blood donors or recipients.

When we access it: Only when you use location-based features like finding donation centers or when you enable location sharing.

Your control: You can disable location access in your device settings at any time.

4.2 Camera Access (CAMERA)

Why we need it: To allow you to take and upload profile pictures or photos related to blood donation activities.

When we access it: Only when you choose to take a photo within the app.

Your control: Camera access is only activated when you tap camera-related buttons.

4.3 Storage Access (READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE, WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE)

Why we need it: To save and access photos from your device's gallery for profile pictures.

When we access it: Only when you choose to upload images from your gallery.

Your control: You can revoke storage permissions in your device settings.

4.4 Notifications (NOTIFICATIONS)

Why we need it: To send you important alerts about urgent blood donation requests and app updates.

When we use it: For urgent blood donation requests, appointment reminders, and important safety information.

Your control: You can disable notifications in your device settings or within the app.

5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With other users: Basic profile information when facilitating blood donation connections
  • With healthcare providers: When necessary for blood donation coordination (with your consent)
  • For legal compliance: When required by law or to protect rights and safety
  • Service providers: With trusted third parties who help us operate the app (under strict confidentiality agreements)

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure servers and databases
  • Regular security audits
  • Limited access to personal information

7. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time using our Data Deletion Request Form.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and data - Request deletion here
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Export your data
